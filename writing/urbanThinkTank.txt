          <p>
           ‘Interdisciplinary’ is often described as the collaboration between different fields—a synthesis of professions in order to usher in new perspectives. In general, the term  can bring to mind marketing jargon, or the pseudo-intellectual. Yet, in the context of Urban Think-Tank, the Caracas and Zurich-based ‘interdisciplinary design studio’, the word symbolises the broader ethical role architects can play in built outcomes. ★
