        In a moment when the very idea of reality was challenged, "Fake Fiction" offered a critical understanding of the present; a double negative disconnected from the real and its often unfathomable nature. What is presented in this journal through the term <i>Fake Fiction</i> is an attempt to bring into question architecture, and its representation, through a negation of its fiction. ★
